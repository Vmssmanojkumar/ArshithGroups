Our Journey

Tracing Our Evolution

From a humble visionary concept to a leading multi-sector corporate brand, see how we have scaled our services over the years.

2019

Suntech Solutions Evolution

Laying the foundation of the group by establishing Suntech Solutions, focusing on business consulting, corporate services, and operational optimization to empower early enterprise growth.

2021

Suntech Expansion with Backend Support

Expanded Suntech Solutions' service portfolio to offer robust backend support, administration management, and initial digital marketing solutions to help clients streamline remote operations.

2023

Arshith Group Evolution.

Consolidating our expanding service operations and consulting divisions under a unified corporate identity, establishing a strong foundation for long-term multi-sector growth and sustainable business development.

2025

Arshith Fresh India Pvt Ltd Inauaguration

Officially incorporating and inaugurating Arshith Fresh India Pvt Ltd, establishing our flagship e-commerce platform to connect farmers directly with consumers.

2026

Arshith Info-Tech Expansion.

Scaling our technical capabilities globally, offering comprehensive software solutions, advanced cloud architectures, and digital transformations to enterprise-grade clients.
